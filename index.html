Biography

Hello! I am Mingye Xie (谢铭烨). I am currently pursing Ph. D. degree in computer science and technology from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I have received the B. Sc. degree and the M. Eng. Degree in electronics and communications engineering from East China Normal University.

My main research topics are computer vision and machine learning. Specifically, I am currently doing research on Generative Adversial Network (GAN), adversarial attack and model compressing. I am also broadly interested in embedding systems programming.
