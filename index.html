Biography

Hello! I am Mingye Xie (谢铭烨), a researcher at the Student Innovation Center of Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I have received Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research primarily focuses on computer vision and embedding systems, with a particular emphasis on camouflage pattern generation, Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), and adversarial attacks.

您好！我是谢铭烨，现任上海交通大学学生创新中心实验师。我于上海交通大学获得计算机科学与技术专业博士学位。

我的研究方向主要集中在计算机视觉和嵌入式系统，目前专注于伪装图案生成、生成对抗网络（GAN）以及对抗攻击等课题。
